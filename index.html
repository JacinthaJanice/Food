1200 Calorie Diet Plan

A balanced, nutritious daily meal guide to help you reach your goals.

1200 kcal / day
☀️

Breakfast

~300 kcal
  • 1 cup oatmeal with skimmed milk
  • ½ banana, sliced
  • 1 boiled egg
  • 1 cup green tea (unsweetened)
🍎

Morning Snack

~100 kcal
  • 1 small apple or pear
  • 5 almonds
🌞

Lunch

~400 kcal
  • ¾ cup brown rice or 1 small whole-wheat wrap
  • 100 g grilled chicken breast or tofu
  • 1 cup steamed broccoli & carrots
  • 1 tsp olive oil dressing
🥗

Afternoon Snack

~100 kcal
  • ½ cup low-fat Greek yoghurt
  • A few berries or cucumber slices
🌙

Dinner

~300 kcal
  • 1 cup lentil or vegetable soup
  • 85 g baked fish (salmon or cod)
  • Large mixed-leaf salad, lemon dressing
  • ½ cup cooked quinoa

💡 Helpful Tips

  1. Drink at least 8 glasses of water throughout the day.
  2. Eat slowly and mindfully — it takes ~20 minutes for fullness signals to reach your brain.
  3. Prep meals in advance to avoid reaching for high-calorie convenience foods.
  4. Pair the plan with 30 minutes of light exercise most days for best results.
  5. Consult a registered dietitian before making major dietary changes.
